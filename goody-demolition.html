Goody Demolition

Goody Demolition is demolition, remediation and haulage company based in the East Kent area. They needed a website design to showcase the many projects they have carried out and also to provide information about the services they offer. With an extensive library of photos available, they were keen for the homepage to make use of full size imagery to provide the "wow factor". Due to large number of projects they're involved with, it was important to create a clearly structured way of organising and navigating their case studies.

In order for the client to carry out updates on a regular basis, the website was built using the Wordpress platform. I provided the designs for the project and the development of the site was carried out by an associate.
