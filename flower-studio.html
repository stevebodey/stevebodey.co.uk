The Flower Studio

The Flower Studio is an established, independent florist based in Canterbury. In the face of increasing online competition, they decided to upgrade their existing web presence whilst also offering customers the option to purchase online. The light and clean design utilises The Flower Studio's existing logo and introduces elements of their corporate colours. The website is a mixture of ecommerce and portfolio pages to reflect the fact that not all of their products can be ordered online.

To provide the client with a user-friendly solution for content and product management, the website has been built around the Magento ecommerce platform. The development of the site was handled by an associate, working to designs that I provided.
