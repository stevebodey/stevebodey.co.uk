Drift Cycles

The Drift Cycles website was developed as an online presence and shop for my own mountain bike retail company. Built around the adaptable Magento ecommerce software, it featured many enhancements including the ability to easily synchronise products with online marketplaces such as eBay and Amazon.

All branding, design and development was carried out by myself and through the website I gained valuable experience of online marketing and the use of Google Analytics and Merchant Center.
