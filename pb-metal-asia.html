P & B Metal Asia

P & B Metal is a metal component manufacturer whose head office is based in Whitstable. Through their marketing company, P & B Metal specified the need for a micro site relating to their factory based in Malaysia. The brief was to create a design that was similar to their existing website, but with an updated look and more emphasis on the high quality imagery available. The colour scheme was based on the existing logo and branding of P & B's Malaysian operations.

The micro site is currently in development with the marketing company.
