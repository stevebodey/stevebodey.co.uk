The Motorist Centre

The Motorist Centre is a small independent garage based near Canterbury. Having recently moved premises, they required a new website based on their existing branding. The clean and simple design in their business colours makes use of a large amount of supplied imagery, as requested by the client.

The Motorist Centre asked for a content management system, so the website has been built around the Wordpress platform. In addition, they specified the need to occasionally advertise vehicles for sale. The WooCommerce ecommerce plugin was added to the Wordpress install to add this functionality in a user-friendly way. The Wordpress template is fully responsive to meet the needs of users accessing the site on a variety of devices.
