Ecologia Environmental

Ecologia Environmental is an environmental consultancy and contractor with offices based throughout the UK and Europe. The client requested a new design that would effectively organise the large amount of content their website contained. In addition, they required a content management system to allow them to manage the site in-house and make amendments where necessary.

The homepage design used elements of colour from the companies branding and the client requested a change in colour scheme for each individual area of expertise. The website has been developed around the Wordpress CMS platform by an associate, working to designs created by myself.
