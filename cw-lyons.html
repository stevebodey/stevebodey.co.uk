C.W. Lyons & Son

C. W. Lyons & Son is a family owned funeral directors located in Canterbury and Whitstable. In the changing face of technology and devices that are used online, the client felt that their current site would benefit from a redesign to make it more accessible. This mainly focussed on the introduction of a responsive design, but also on the structure of their page navigation.

Working alongside their existing marketing agency, the client decided that their new design should be uplifting and calming, using large imagery with a hint of magenta taken from their current website.

The website is currently in development with the marketing company.
