Destination Triumph

Destination Triumph is a group of three separate Triumph Motorcycles dealer locations based in the south of England. Having recently acquired their third location, the group wanted to develop a new website to offer new and used motorcycles to their customer base whilst also offering information on parts/servicing and location details.

The brief was to create an eye-catching design based around the brand guidelines and corporate colours of the Triumph Motorcycle Company. As the site was to be quite extensive, emphasis was placed on clear and intuitive navigation. Also, much thought was given on how to present the detailed information presented in the used motorcycle section.

The design was created while working for GForces Web Management and the website was powered by their industry leading NetDirector software.
