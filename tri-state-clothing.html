Tri-State Clothing

Tri-State Clothing is a locally based action sports clothing company producing unique and individual designs. They wanted to establish an online presence and also provide customers with the facility to purchase clothing and accessories through their website. Having strong lifestyle imagery and striking product shots the minimalist design and colour palette was developed to showcase these elements.

The ecommerce functionality is provided by the flexible Magento platform and the website has been developed from my designs by an associate.
