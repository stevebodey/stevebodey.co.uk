K&G Hairdressing Supplies

K&G Hairdressing Supplies are based in Ashford and provide professional hairdressing supplies to Salons. They recently had an ecommerce site produced using the WooCommerce plugin for WordPress, but were unhappy with the design of the theme.

I mocked up a homepage layout for the existing website, to show how I would improve on their current design. Previously, the homepage was cluttered and lacked definition between it's different sections - the main focus of the site, the product catalogue, was given little prominence. I chose to give more focus to the product categories by adding them to the main menu and creating jump points high on the homepage. I retained their original theme colour, but chose a darker shade of blue to give a more luxurious feel to the site which better reflects the hairdressing industry.
