Soapbox Training Trust

The SoapBox Training Trust is a charitable organisation that provides Food Safety in Catering courses and DBS Disclosures. They required a simple but effective website to clearly display information about the services they offer. The design was based around their existing branding/colours and made use of free stock imagery to keep costs to a minimum.

The website is responsive and has been built around the Bootstrap framework to ensure that it offers the best experience to all visitors, no matter what device they use to access it.
